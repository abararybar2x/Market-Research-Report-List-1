<p><p>The Peripheral Arterial Interventional Devices market comprises key types, including balloon dilatation catheters and stents. Balloon dilatation catheters are used to treat narrowed arteries by inflating a small balloon at the site of blockage, which widens the artery and improves blood flow. Stents are mesh-like structures implanted to keep arteries open post-dilation, providing structural support and reducing the risk of re-narrowing. Together, these devices play a crucial role in managing peripheral arterial diseases and enhancing patient outcomes.</p></p>
