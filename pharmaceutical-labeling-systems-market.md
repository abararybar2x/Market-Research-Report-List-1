<p><p>Pharmaceutical labeling systems can be categorized into vertical and horizontal labeling machines, each serving specific packaging needs. Vertical labeling machines apply labels to products in an upright position, ideal for bottles and vials, ensuring efficient labeling at high speeds. In contrast, horizontal wrap labeling machines are designed to wrap labels around cylindrical products, accommodating a variety of shapes and sizes. Both types are crucial in the pharmaceutical industry for ensuring compliance with regulations and enhancing product identification and branding.</p></p>
