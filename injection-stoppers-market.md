<p><p>The injection stoppers market comprises various types of materials such as butyl rubber, chlorobutyl rubber, and bromobutyl rubber. Butyl rubber is known for its excellent impermeability and flexibility, making it suitable for medical and pharmaceutical applications. Chlorobutyl rubber offers improved chemical resistance and is often used in parenteral packaging. Bromobutyl rubber enhances barrier properties and thermal stability, ideal for high-temperature sterilization processes. Each type serves specific requirements in protecting the integrity of injectable products and ensuring safety in their storage and usage.</p></p>
