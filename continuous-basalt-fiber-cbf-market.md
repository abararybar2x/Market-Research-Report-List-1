<p><p>Continuous Basalt Fiber (CBF) is a type of advanced material derived from basalt rock, known for its high strength, thermal stability, and resistance to chemical corrosion. The CBF market includes various manufacturing processes: Centrifugal-Blowing involves spinning molten basalt fibers through a nozzle, creating fine fibers suitable for lightweight applications. Centrifugal-Multiroll employs multiple rollers to produce thicker, more robust fibers for structural uses. Die-Blowing utilizes a die to extrude fibers, focusing on specific shapes and sizes for diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
