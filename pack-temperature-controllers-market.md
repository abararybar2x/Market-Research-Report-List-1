<p><p>Pack temperature controllers regulate temperature in various applications by using different control methods. On-Off controllers provide simple, binary temperature management, turning heating or cooling devices on or off. Autotuned PID controllers use algorithms to optimize control based on real-time data, enhancing precision. Multiloop controllers manage multiple zones or processes simultaneously for complex systems. Safety limit controllers ensure temperatures remain within set safety thresholds to prevent hazards. Other types may include adaptive controllers or those designed for specialized applications.</p></p>
