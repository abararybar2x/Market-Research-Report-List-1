<p><p>The OPF (Organic Photodetector Film) CMOS sensors market is primarily divided into two types: linear image sensors and area image sensors. Linear image sensors capture images one line at a time, making them suitable for applications like barcode scanning and high-speed imaging. In contrast, area image sensors capture an entire frame simultaneously, ideal for digital cameras and surveillance systems. Both types utilize advanced organic materials to enhance sensitivity and efficiency in various imaging scenarios.</p></p>
