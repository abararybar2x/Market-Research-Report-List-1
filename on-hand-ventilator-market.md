<p><p>The on-hand ventilator market can be categorized into three primary types: electronic, pneumatic, and electro-pneumatic ventilators. Electronic ventilators utilize digital technology for precise control and monitoring of airflow and patient parameters, enhancing versatility and adaptability. Pneumatic ventilators operate using compressed air, offering simplicity and reliability, often in emergency situations. Electro-pneumatic ventilators combine both electronic and pneumatic systems, providing the advantages of both technologies for optimal ventilation support, suitable for various clinical environments and patient needs.</p></p>
