<p><p>Self-expanding coronary stents are medical devices used to treat narrowed or blocked coronary arteries. These stents are designed to expand upon deployment, helping to restore blood flow to the heart. Their self-expanding nature, typically achieved through materials like nitinol, allows for better adaptability to varying arterial sizes, improving patient outcomes and reducing the risk of re-narrowing.</p><p>The self-expanding coronary stents market is experiencing significant growth, driven by the increasing prevalence of cardiovascular diseases and advancements in stent technology. Innovations such as drug-eluting stents, which release medication to prevent restenosis, are contributing to this upward trend. Additionally, the growing elderly population and rising awareness about cardiovascular health are further fueling market expansion.</p><p>As healthcare providers seek effective solutions for complex coronary artery issues, the demand for self-expanding stents continues to rise.
