<p><p>Air filter media encompasses various grades designed for different filtration efficiencies. HEPA (High-Efficiency Particulate Air) grade media captures at least 99.97% of particles 0.3 microns in size, making it suitable for cleanrooms and medical environments. MERV (Minimum Efficiency Reporting Value) grade media ranges from 1 to 16, with higher ratings filtering smaller particles, ideal for residential and commercial applications. ULPA (Ultra-Low Particulate Air) grade media filters 99.999% of particles, enhancing protection in critical environments like labs and manufacturing facilities.</p></p>
