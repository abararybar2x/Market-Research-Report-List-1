<p><ul><li>Single-component Aqueous Polyurethane</li><li>Bicomponent Aqueous Polyurethane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Non-ionic Aqueous Polyurethane market is primarily segmented into two types: single-component and bicomponent aqueous polyurethane. Single-component aqueous polyurethane features a ready-to-use formulation that requires no additional hardener, making it convenient for applications. In contrast, bicomponent aqueous polyurethane consists of two separate components—resin and hardener—that must be mixed before application, offering enhanced chemical resistance and durability. Both types cater to diverse industries, including coatings, adhesives, and textiles, primarily for their eco-friendly characteristics and versatility.</p></p>
