<p><p>The Laser Scored Bag market includes several types based on scoring techniques. Inline Laser Scoring involves cutting the bag material during production, ensuring precision and efficiency. Transverse Laser Scoring creates crosswise cuts for easy opening. Freeform Laser Scoring allows for custom patterns, enhancing aesthetics and functionality. Micro-perforation Scoring produces tiny holes for controlled airflow and moisture escape. Other categories may encompass innovative scoring techniques or hybrid methods, catering to diverse customer needs and packaging applications in various industries.</p></p>
