<p><ul><li>Blow Molding</li><li>Injection Molding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The HDPE blow molded products market is primarily segmented into blow molding and injection molding. Blow molding involves inflating hot plastic within a mold to create hollow objects, ideal for bottles and containers. Injection molding, on the other hand, injects molten HDPE into a mold, allowing for precise shapes and greater design flexibility, often used for smaller, solid items. Both processes leverage high-density polyethylene's strength and durability, catering to diverse industries such as packaging, automotive, and consumer goods.</p></p>
