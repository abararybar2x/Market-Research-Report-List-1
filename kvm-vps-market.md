<p><p>KVM (Kernel-based Virtual Machine) and VPS (Virtual Private Server) are pivotal technologies in the virtualization landscape, enabling efficient resource allocation and management. VPS, on the other hand, is a virtualized server that mimics a dedicated server within a shared hosting environment, providing users with dedicated resources and root access for customization.</p><p>The KVM & VPS market is witnessing significant growth, driven by the increasing demand for cloud-based services, cost efficiency, and scalable solutions suitable for both small and large enterprises.
