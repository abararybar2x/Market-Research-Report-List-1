<p><p>The neurodiagnostic and monitoring devices market encompasses various technologies designed to evaluate and monitor neurological functions. Electroencephalogram (EEG) devices measure electrical activity in the brain, helping diagnose conditions like epilepsy. Electromyography (EMG) assesses muscle electrical activity, aiding in neuromuscular disorder diagnosis. Transcranial Doppler monitors blood flow in the brain's major arteries, detecting potential strokes. Intracranial pressure monitors gauge pressure within the skull, crucial for managing traumatic brain injuries. Cerebral oximeters track oxygen levels in the brain, enhancing patient care during critical situations.</p></p>
