<p><ul><li>Lipoprotein Test</li><li>Angiotensin Test</li><li>Fibrinogen Test</li><li>Serum Uric Acid Test</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cardiovascular diagnostic testing market includes various specialized tests that assess heart health and associated risk factors. The Lipoprotein Test measures cholesterol levels, helping identify risks of heart disease. The Angiotensin Test evaluates renin-angiotensin system activity, indicating hypertension risk. The Fibrinogen Test measures blood clotting levels, providing insights into thrombosis risk. Lastly, the Serum Uric Acid Test identifies elevated uric acid levels, which can indicate cardiovascular and metabolic conditions. Collectively, these tests aid in early detection and prevention strategies in cardiovascular care.</p></p>
