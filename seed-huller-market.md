<p><strong>The Seed Huller Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Disc Hullers</li><li>Impact Hullers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The seed huller market features various types, primarily disc hullers and impact hullers. Disc hullers utilize rotating discs to separate seeds from their husks, ensuring minimal damage to the seeds, making them ideal for delicate varieties. Conversely, impact hullers rely on high-speed impacts to efficiently remove husks, often used for tougher seeds due to their robust design. Both types cater to different agricultural needs, influencing the choice of technology based on seed type, yield requirements, and operational efficiency.</p></p>
