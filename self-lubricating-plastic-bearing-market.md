<p><p>The self-lubricating plastic bearing market encompasses two main types: plastic rolling bearings and plastic sliding bearings. Plastic rolling bearings utilize balls or rollers made from plastic to reduce friction, offering advantages in weight and corrosion resistance, making them suitable for high-speed applications. On the other hand, plastic sliding bearings rely on a sliding motion between two surfaces, providing wear resistance and low friction without the need for additional lubrication. Both types are increasingly popular in various industries for their durability and maintenance-free operation.</p></p>
