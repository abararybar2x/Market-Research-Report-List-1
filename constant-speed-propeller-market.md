<p><p>The constant speed propeller market is divided into two main types: hydraulic and electric propellers. Hydraulic propellers utilize a fluid mechanism to adjust blade pitch, ensuring optimal performance across various speeds and improving fuel efficiency. They are commonly used in larger vessels. Electric propellers, on the other hand, leverage electric motors for pitch adjustment, offering precise control and reduced maintenance needs. These are becoming increasingly popular due to their eco-friendliness and suitability for modern applications, including electric and hybrid vessels.</p></p>
