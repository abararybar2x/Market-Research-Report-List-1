<p><p>The postoperative pain management market can be categorized into three main types: acute, moderate, and severe pain management. Acute pain management focuses on immediate post-surgical discomfort, utilizing medications like opioids and non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs) for short-term relief. Moderate pain management involves longer-term therapies, including oral analgesics and adjunct treatments. Severe pain management addresses intense pain levels, often requiring stronger opioid prescriptions or multimodal approaches, including nerve blocks and patient-controlled analgesia, to enhance patient recovery and comfort.</p></p>
