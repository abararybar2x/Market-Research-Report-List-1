<p><p>The Mid-Frequency Transformers market can be segmented into three cooling types: Free Convection, Air Cooling, and Water Cooling. Free Convection utilizes natural airflow to dissipate heat, making it cost-effective and low-maintenance. Air Cooling employs fans to enhance airflow and manage heat, ensuring efficient operation in various environments. Water Cooling offers superior heat removal through circulating water, making it suitable for high-capacity transformers requiring optimal temperature control. Each type serves distinct industrial applications based on cooling efficiency and operational requirements.</p></p>
