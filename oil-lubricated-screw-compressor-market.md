<p><p>The oil-lubricated screw compressor market is primarily segmented into floor-mounted and tank-mounted types. Floor-mounted compressors are designed for direct installation on the ground, offering easy access for maintenance and space efficiency in industrial settings. In contrast, tank-mounted compressors are integrated with an air receiver tank, allowing for compressed air storage, which enhances system efficiency and reduces cycling rates. Both types are widely used across various industries, providing reliable and continuous air supply for numerous applications.</p></p>
