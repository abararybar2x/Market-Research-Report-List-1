<p><ul><li>Single Channel</li><li>Multi-Channel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial propellers market can be categorized into two main types: single-channel and multi-channel propellers. Single-channel propellers are designed to deliver efficient propulsion through a single flow path, making them suitable for specific applications like pumps and turbines. In contrast, multi-channel propellers feature several flow paths, enhancing performance and efficiency in larger systems like marine vessels and industrial machinery. The choice between these types depends on the application's requirements, including flow rate, efficiency, and operating conditions.</p></p>
