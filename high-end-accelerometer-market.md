<p><p>The high-end accelerometer market is categorized into three main types based on their axis sensitivity: 1-axis, 2-axis, and 3-axis accelerometers. </p><p>1-axis accelerometers measure acceleration along a single direction, making them suitable for simple applications like tilt sensing. </p><p>2-axis accelerometers capture motion across two perpendicular axes, ideal for tracking movements in more complex devices. </p><p>3-axis accelerometers provide comprehensive motion sensing by measuring acceleration along three dimensions, widely used in smartphones, gaming devices, and advanced automotive systems.</p></p>
