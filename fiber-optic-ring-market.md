<p><p>The Fiber Optic Ring market is segmented into two primary types: Single-Mode Fiber Optic Rings and Multimode Fiber Optic Rings. Single-Mode Fiber Optic Rings utilize a single light path for long-distance communication, boasting higher bandwidth and reduced signal loss, making them ideal for telecommunications and data centers. In contrast, Multimode Fiber Optic Rings support multiple light paths, suitable for shorter distances and applications like local area networks. Both types enhance data transmission efficiency and performance across various industries.</p></p>
