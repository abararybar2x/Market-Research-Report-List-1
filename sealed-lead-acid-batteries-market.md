<p><p>The Sealed Lead-Acid Battery market includes three main types: Absorbent Glass Mat (AGM) batteries, Gel batteries, and other variants. AGM batteries utilize a fiberglass mat to absorb electrolyte, enhancing reliability and vibration resistance, making them ideal for applications like motorcycles and UPS systems. Gel batteries contain a silica-based gel that immobilizes the electrolyte, offering superior deep cycle performance and reduced risk of leakage. Other battery types may include traditional sealed lead-acid options suitable for various industrial and consumer applications.</p></p>
