<p><p>The Titanium Aluminide Alloy market can be categorized into two main types: Gamma Type and Other Type. Gamma Type alloys, primarily comprised of titanium and aluminum, exhibit high strength and excellent oxidation resistance, making them ideal for high-temperature applications in aerospace and automotive industries.
